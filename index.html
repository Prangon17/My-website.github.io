About Me

I am A.K.M. Atiqur Rahman, and I earned my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Green University of Bangladesh in 2024. With a CGPA of 3.92 out of 4.00, I ranked 1st in my graduating batch. My dedication to education has fueled my aspirations for higher studies abroad, ideally with a scholarship, to expand my knowledge and skills further.

During my undergraduate journey, I actively served as a student mentor and mentor advisor, providing guidance and support to my peers. I also took on the role of treasurer for the IEEE GUB Student Branch, where I contributed to various initiatives and events.

My passion for research drives my involvement in the Green Computing and Communication (GCC) research group within my department. My research interests encompass optimization, artificial intelligence, deep learning, machine learning-based approaches in wireless sensor networks, UAV systems, multi-agent systems, the Internet of Things (IoT), and mobile edge computing. Additionally, I am an active member of the IEEE Computer Society, where I engage with fellow professionals and stay updated on the latest advancements in the field.
